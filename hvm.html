    Hostile Vehicle Mitigation

    Reducing Risks and Protecting Against Vehicle-Borne Threats

    Overview

    Hostile Vehicle Mitigation (HVM) is a protective security discipline that focuses on reducing risks associated with vehicle-borne threats. These range from vandalism to sophisticated or aggressive attacks by determined criminals or terrorists. HVM integrates advanced solutions to prioritize the safety of people and infrastructure.

    Key Benefits of HVM

    Improved Security

    State-of-the-art measures to protect against unauthorized vehicle access.

    Traffic Management

    Streamlined traffic flow and access control for high-security areas.

    Enhanced Safety

    Compliance with safety standards and reduced risk of vehicle-borne incidents.

    Features of Our HVM Systems

    • Anti-ram barriers and bollards
    • Access control and monitoring systems
    • Crash-rated gates and fencing
    • Integration with advanced surveillance
    • Customizable solutions for diverse threats
